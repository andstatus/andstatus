AndStatus

A low traffic social networking client with tree-like threaded conversations for Android.

About

AndStatus is a low traffic social networking client with tree-like threaded conversations. It supports multiple protocols, including GNU social (e.g. Quitter, LoadAverage, Vinilox, etc.), Twitter and Pump.io.

Features

  • Combines your accounts from all networks into one Timeline
  • Allows you reading and posting even while you are offline.
  • For Android OS version 4.1 or higher.
  • Under Free/Libre and Open Source License.

    See the Change Log for descriptions of all the features of AndStatus and links to discussions of the features. For more information please read the AndStatus Wiki.

    Development

    AndStatus is an open project:

    • Open for user's ideas, problems and questions. Please start from searching known problems, solutions and ideas at the "Issues" page. Take part in discussions there. If nothing relevant was found, raise your "issue" sharing with the AndStatus community your idea, question or a problem (providing as much details as possible in order to understand and reproduce the problem).
    • Multilingual. Crowdsourcing and collaborative localization project invites volunteer translators: AndStatus at Crowdin.
    • Open Source, open for contributing developers. If you are a Developer, AndStatus project will help you implementing your unique Social networking features, because you can build on working code supported by a suite of automated tests. The code is commented and has links to related information on Internet. Please read the Developer FAQ page for more info on compiling AndStatus.
